1 run consists of an array of 12 slurm jobs. 
	Each job has . . .
		8 batches of data. 
			Each batch of data has 1000 input matrices.
		The 8 batches of data are passed to 24 workers (8 batches x 3 L0 penalty values).
		The 24 workers run until they have colletively run 50,000 timesteps.
	The run has a total of 600,000 timesteps over 96,000 input matrices, over 3 L0 values.
